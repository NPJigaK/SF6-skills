# Drive System

## Summary

The Drive System is described by the source as a Street Fighter 6 meter system
that gates universal movement, offense, and defense mechanics.

## Definition

The source glossary lists these Drive-related terms:

- Burnout
- Drive Impact
- Drive Parry
- Drive Reversal
- Drive Rush
- Drive System
- Overdrive

## Why it matters

The Drive System is a central organizing concept for interpreting Street
Fighter 6 mechanics because the source connects it to movement, offense,
defense, and resource exhaustion.
