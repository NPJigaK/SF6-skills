# Ryu

## Summary

Ryu is the Street Fighter 6 character covered by the second official
frame-data capture in this wiki.

## Timeline

| Date | Event | Source |
|---|---|---|
| 2026-05-27 | Official Ryu frame-data page captured for Classic and Modern controls. | [[sources/capcom-official-ryu-frame-data]] |

## Relevant claims

- The official capture contains 75 Classic frame-data rows and 69 Modern
  frame-data rows.
- The capture keeps raw input token data, including controller icons and
  Modern-control tokens.
- This page does not yet summarize individual move values; those remain in the
  derived CSVs and raw DOM artifacts.
- The capture was reviewed and accepted by the human reviewer.
