# Zangief

## Summary

Zangief is the Street Fighter 6 character covered by the fourth official
frame-data capture in this wiki.

## Relevant claims

- The official capture contains 72 Classic frame-data rows and 66 Modern
  frame-data rows.
- The capture keeps raw input token data, including controller icons,
  Modern-control tokens, and one/two-circle command tokens.
- This page does not yet summarize individual move values; those remain in the
  derived CSVs and raw DOM artifacts.
- The capture was reviewed and accepted by the human reviewer.
