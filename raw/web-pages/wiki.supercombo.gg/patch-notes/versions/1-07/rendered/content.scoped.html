Terry Patch

  • Terry released


Universal Changes

  • Drive Impact
    • Fixed issue where the proximity guard hitbox did not match the full attack hitbox range (A.K.I., Chun-Li, Dee Jay, Kimberly, Luke)
    • Fixed issues where absorbing an attack would affect the DI properties for some characters
      • Blanka: proximity guard hitbox was shorter than usual, and the range on 2nd active frame was longer than usual
      • Dee Jay: attack hitbox range became shorter than usual
      • Kimberly: proximity guard hitbox would disappear on frames 1-17
  • Drive Reversal
    • Expanded the throw hurtbox to ensure all characters can punish with a throw at close range
    • Because some characters lacked strong punish options at -6 due to Light normal starter scaling, this gives a universal option for meterless damage and Drive gauge depletion
  • Perfect Parry
    • Fixed issue where, against specific projectile attacks with screen freeze properties, the player would incur the 10-frame recovery of a non-freeze Perfect Parry
    • Applies to 2nd hit onward for JP's Stribog (236P) and Rashid's Arabian Cyclone (214P)
  • Modern Controls
    • Fixed issue where 20% shortcut scaling would apply to manually input special moves during a screen freeze (e.g. Super freeze)
  • Assist Combos
    • Fixed issues when entering Burnout during certain Assist Combos; using the same Assist Combo during the recovery or buffer window of the previous move would prevent the 2nd hit of the sequence from coming out
    • Applies to Assist Combo 2 for Dhalsim, Juri, Luke, Kimberly, and Manon; Assist Combo 3 for JP, Juri, and Manon
