Version2.0111
Game Version2.0111
dateDecember 16, 2025
SummaryBug fixes and minor adjustments, primarily for Elena, Mai, and Sagat.
LinksPatch Notes


Universal Changes

  • Drive Gauge Appearance: if any amount of Drive gauge remains over the threshold (0/10000/20000/30000/40000/50000), the entire block of gauge will change color to make it easier to distinguish from an empty block
  • Command Throw SA3/CA (Lily, Manon, Zangief): removed unnecessary block idle hitbox that allowed opponents to avoid the throw when landing from a jump
  • 2LK (all characters): extended foot hurtbox that appears before active frames is now invincible to cross-up attacks


Character Changes

C. Viper

  • j.LP / j.HP no longer Force Stand when hitting a crouching opponent
  • Lv.3 Focus Force (214[K]/[KK]) now deals +4 additional hitstun on Punish Counter like the uncharged versions
    • Now KD +77, and crumple is +29 before opponent falls to the ground


Chun-Li

  • Serenity Stream > LP / LK / MK on Perfect Parry: removed some recovery frames immediately before her forced Stance Exit
    • This reduces the total recovery on PP by 2f (LP) / 5f (LK) / 6f (MK) and affects Perfect Parry disadvantage by the same amount
    • This was done because despite being in a Punish Counter state, Chun-Li could block in the middle of her recovery animation on these frames.


Dhalsim

  • CA fixed a bug where the Drive Damage and Chip Damage were set to SA3 levels when canceled into
    • Drive Damage on Block: 7500 → 10000
    • Chip Damage: 1000 → 1250


Elena

  • Leopard Snap (236P~6LK) Super Art gain 600 → 1000
  • Boosted Leopard Snap (236P~6P~6LK or 236PP~6LK) pushback on block reduced, and normalized the distance on block when used midscreen or in the corner
    • 1st hit has less blowback on airborne opponents to make the 2nd hit more consistent in juggles
  • Moon Glider (214P~6P) 2nd attack hitbox expanded forward
  • OD Moon Glider (214PP) pushback on hit reduced, increased forward movement after the backwards sway
    • More effective as a shimmy tool, leading to more consistent Punish Counter follow-ups
  • SA1 startup reduced 9f → 7f; total animation 96f → 94f; can now build Super gauge after landing this in a combo
    • All airborne and air invuln frames shifted 2f earlier to compensate


Mai

  • Forward Throw leaves Mai closer to a cornered opponent (can no longer walk back to shimmy after a dash)
  • 5HP Drive Damage reduced 5000 → 4000
  • Kachousen (236P) - Charged / Charged OD / Flame versions build more Drive gauge for the defender when Parried and/or Perfect Parried

Sagat

  • 2LK pushback reduced on hit/block for more consistent hitconfirms
  • 2MK Hit Advantage +4 → +5; hitstun 25 → 26
    • Gives a natural link to 5LP, a CH link to 5LK/2MP, and a DR/PC link to itself at farther ranges
  • Tiger Monolith (4HP) 1st hit active frames increased 2 → 3 (more consistent juggle tool, does not affect anti-air frames)
  • Charged HP Tiger Uppercut (623[HP]) now gives the stronger cinematic hit up to the 3rd active frame instead of only frames 1-2; collision box expanded upward on active frames 1-2
    • This slightly increases the max range of the strong hit and makes it more consistent in some high juggles
  • HK Tiger Knee (236HK) expanded combo-only hitbox of 2nd hit upward to prevent it from whiffing when 1st hit connects (does not apply to "sweet spot" connect)
  • LK/MK Tiger Nexus (214LK/214MK) increased pushback on hit; increased forward movement during startup; increased horizontal blowback on airborne hit
    • Total range 1.56 → 1.66 (214LK) / 1.86 → 1.96 (214MK)
    • More consistent in grounded confirms (e.g. 3 lights > 214LK), while keeping juggle scenarios intact
  • Mighty Tiger (214K/KK~6LK) increased pushback on hit; increased horizontal blowback on airborne hit
    • If 214LK~6LK is used from a 2-light confirm, opponent is in range for Throw or 2LK~5LP mixup; does not work from new 3-light confirm
  • Greedy Tiger (214K/KK~6MK) Drive Damage increased 6000 → 8000
  • Nova Tiger (214K/KK~6HK) reduced pushback on block
    • Can now throw opponents who back walk, but can no longer shimmy unless opponent delays throw tech at least 3f
