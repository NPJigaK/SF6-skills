Version1.05
Game Version1.05
dateMay 22, 2024
SummaryAkuma Release. Balance changes for all characters.
LinksPatch Notes


Akuma Patch

  • Akuma released


Universal Changes

  • Damage Scaling on grounded light normals increased 10% → 20%
  • Back Throw oki has generally been weakened when throwing opponent into the corner
  • Drive Reversal on block -8 → -6 and can no longer be low-profiled
    • Can now be input on wakeup by holding 6HPHK; this version is 2f faster (18f) and has no screen freeze
  • Drive Parry:
    • Throw hurtbox expanded; strike/projectile hurtbox expanded from frame 3~
    • Minimum parry time increased by 8f total (extra 4f active + 4f recovery)
    • After a successful parry, Drive gauge starts to regenerate after 20f instead of 60f
  • Perfect Parry now inflicts scaling to meter gain and Drive damage for the entire punish combo
    • 20% scaling on Super gauge build for both characters
    • 50% scaling on defender's Drive damage and on attacker's Drive gain
  • Can no longer override the damage scaling of a Drive Impact Stun performed at the max distance from corner (bug fix)
  • 1st startup frame of SA1 is now strike/throw invuln instead of full invuln for all characters that have invincibility

  • Whirlwind Kick (6HK) increased pushback on block (pushback now also applies to Ryu when opponent is cornered; much easier to space safely)
    • Foot hurtbox reduced upward; Low Crush lasts 1f longer (13-19f → 13-20f)
  • Fuwa Triple Strike 1 (5MP~LK) Block Advantage -7 → -4; no longer cancels to HK ender on whiff
  • Fuwa Triple Strike 2 (5MP~LK~HK) now cancelable into Denjin Charge by inputting 2P (on hit only; KD Adv. +7)
  • High Double Strike (5HP~HK) now cancelable into Denjin Charge by inputting 2P (on hit only; KD Adv. +7)

  • Hadoken (236P) extended hurtbox reduced, making it much harder to counterpoke; +2f hitstun on all versions
    • Hit Advantage: LP -1 → +1, MP -3 → -1, HP -5 → -3
  • Denjin Hadoken (D.236HP) now only comes out when pressing HP; can use regular LP/MP versions while retaining Denjin stock
  • HP Shoryuken (623HP) cancel into SA3 is now guaranteed to get a full cinematic connect (due to 2f startup on SA3)
  • Tatsumaki Senpu-kyaku (214K) increased juggle limit; increased juggle start/increase value (less lenient afterwards)
    • Damage increased on all versions (LK 800 → 900, MK 900 → 1000, HK 1000 → 1200)
    • Super Art gain increased on all versions (LK 1000 → 1200, MK 1000 → 1300, HK 1000 → 1400)
    • Drive gain increased on all versions (LK 2000 → 3000, MK 2000 → 3500, HK 2000 → 4000)
  • OD Aerial Tatsumaki Senpu-kyaku (j.214KK) collision box and juggle-only hitbox expanded to make juggles more consistent
    • Last hit has higher juggle potential and will hit much more consistently

  • LK High Blade Kick (236LK) startup 15f → 14f; active 9 → 6 (total move length 45f → 41f, now combos from 2LP)
    • Damage 1000 → 1100; KD advantage increased +35 → +42 (auto-timed safe jump setup)
    • Super Art gain 1200 → 1400; Drive gain 2000 → 3000; Drive Damage on block 4000 → 6000
  • MK High Blade Kick (236MK) damage 1100 → 1200; Super Art gain 1200 → 1400; Drive gain 3000 → 3500
  • HK High Blade Kick (236HK) Super Art gain 1200 → 1400
  • OD High Blade Kick (236KK) startup 18f → 17f; combo scaling reduced from 10% extra → 5% extra
    • Less pushback when hitting airborne opponents

  • LP Hashogeki (214LP) pushback reduced on hit
  • MP Hashogeki (214MP) pushback reduced on hit; Drive Damage on block 4000 → 6000
  • HP Hashogeki (214HP) Drive Damage on block 4000 → 6000
  • OD Hashogeki (214PP) Drive Damage on block 4000 → 6000
  • Denjin Hashogeki (D.214HP) now only comes out when pressing HP; can use regular LP/MP versions while retaining Denjin stock
    • Startup 21f → 20f (now combos from 2MK); Drive Damage on block 2000 x2 → 3000 x2
    • Recovery (hit/whiff) 18 → 19; KD Advantage +62 → +61
  • Denjin OD Hashogeki (D.214PP) damage 1000 → 800; Drive Damage on block 4000 → 6000

  • SA1 startup 8f → 7f; can now be performed while OD Hadoken is on-screen
    • Expanded combo-only hitbox starting on 3rd active frame (more consistent in combos)
  • SA2 removed projectile clash hitbox; increased damage on Regular/Denjin Lv.1 and Lv.2 versions
    • Normal Lv.1 2600 → 2800; Normal Lv.2 2800 → 2900
    • Denjin Lv.1 3000 → 3200; Denjin Lv.2 3200 → 3300
  • SA3 non-cinematic final hit damage 1000 → 2000 (total: 2600 → 3600); hitbox extended upwards from active frame 3~
  • SA3/CA startup 5f → 2f when canceled from 623HP (ensures combo consistency)


Zangief

  • Improved Super Meter gain on:
    • 5MP, 5MK, 2MP, 2MK
    • 3MP, 6MK, 6HP, 6HK, 3HK, j.8HP
    • PP/PPP, 360+P/PP, j.360+K/KK, Close 63214K/KK, Mid/Far 63214K/KK
  • Improved Drive Gauge gain on:
    • 5[HP], 5MK, 2MP
    • 3MP, 6MK, 6HP, 6HK, 3HK, j.8HP
    • PP, 360+P, Close 63214K
  • Improved Drive Damage (Block/Punish Counter) on:
    • 5[HP], 5MK, 2MP, 2MK (PC only)
    • 6MK (PC only), 6HP

  • 5LK less pushback on hit (improves consistency when canceled)
  • 5MP less pushback on hit
  • 5MK damage 800 → 900; hitbox expanded forward
    • NOTE: the hitbox was reverted to its previous version in Patch 1.06 only 1 month afterwards
  • 2MP less pushback on hit
  • 2MK Hit Advantage +2 → +3 and less pushback (allows link to 3MP after close range Drive Rush)
  • 5[HP] damage 1000 → 1400, armor during active frames expanded upwards on arms
  • 2HP startup 12 → 11, active 8 → 9, Block Advantage -19 → -20
    • Punish Counter puts opponent into limited juggle OTG bounce state; moves forward more on startup
  • 2HK forward hurtbox reduced
  • Knee Hammer (6MK) damage 600 → 700, recovery 14 → 13, Hit Advantage 0 → +1, Block Advantage -4 → -3
    • Pushback on hit reduced; overall, much easier to space for frame advantage, and much stronger out of Drive Rush
    • NOTE: the block advantage was reverted to -4 in Patch 1.07, along with a nerf to its forward hurtbox
  • Headbutt (6HP) damage 900 → 1000, Hit Advantage +6 → +7 (allows important link to 3MP)
  • Cyclone Wheel Kick (6HK) Block Advantage -12 → -9, more pushback, recoils farther on block (much easier to space safely)
    • Expanded hurtbox around feet during recovery (easier to whiff punish)
  • Flying Headbutt (j.8HP) damage 800 → 900
  • Machine Gun Chops (5MP~MP) hitbox expanded forward, less pushback on hit (more combo consistency)
  • Power Stomps (22MK~MK) can now be special canceled

  • Double Lariat (PP) damage 1200 → 1400; final hit puts opponent into free juggle state (useful for stun combos)
  • OD Double Lariat (PPP) 1st hit damage 500 → 700
  • Borscht Dynamite (j.360+K) can now grab opponents in a free juggle state, and combos when canceled from air-to-air j.8HP
    • Hitbox expands to match j.360+KK in juggles; 20% immediate scaling when comboed into
  • OD Siberian Express (63214KK Far) movement speed increased after absorbing attack
  • Tundra Storm (22HK) counter hitbox expanded inward, active frames 2 → 3 on successful counter (works more consistently, especially vs. Drive Reversal)
    • No longer counters if the opponent is behind Zangief
