Universal Changes

  • Jump Cancels
    • Canceling a character's prejump frames into a special move can no longer be used to escape normal Throws as a defensive option
    • Active frames of a normal Throw are extended when they make contact with throw invuln prejump frames, ensuring a jump canceled special will be thrown immediately
    • This is especially impactful for Zangief and Lily, who have a longer prejump time that could be canceled into a command throw
  • Sweeps
    • Several 2HK sweeps has their pushback or recovery adjusted to ensure that opponents can consistently punish them on block
    • Some perfectly spaced sweeps may still be safe in specific matchups when spaced perfectly
    • Applied to Akuma, Cammy, Ken, Jamie, and M. Bison
  • Perfect Parry vs. Multi-hit Attacks
    • After a Perfect Parry, it is no longer possible to cancel the later hits of an attack
    • This was already true on block, but now applies on hit and armor as well
    • This change allows characters to punish more attacks with Drive Impact, such as M. Bison's OD Psycho Crusher and Lily's Windclad OD Condor Spire
      • Video example of how the cancel used to work
      • Note: this change was temporarily reverted due to a bug, but was re-applied in Version 1.09
  • Overdrive Specials
    • The visual effects of OD moves now take priority over Burnout recovery effects when performed immediately after Burnout ends
  • Super Armor
    • Fixed issue where an attack could be absorbed by armor even when connecting with a non-armored hurtbox, if it connected during the hitstop of another attack that was armor absorbed
    • Hypothetical example: Marisa absorbs a projectile with Gladius (upper-body armor), then a 2MK connects immediately afterward during the armor hitstop, leading to it being absorbed when it should go under the armor hurtbox
  • Collision Box Behavior
    • Fixed issue where cornered characters would shift a greater distance when switching sides with an airborne opponent
