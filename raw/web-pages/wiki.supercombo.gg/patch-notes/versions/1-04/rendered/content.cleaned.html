Version1.04
Game Version1.04
dateFebruary 27, 2024
SummaryEd Release. Several balance changes and bug fixes.
LinksPatch Notes


Ed Patch

  • Ed released


Universal Changes

  • Drive Rush
    • Simultaneous activation no longer randomly gives the frame advantage to one player
  • Perfect Parry
    • Fixed issue where a Perfect Parry could occur when absorbing a hit on the 3rd startup frame
  • Armor Break
    • Fixed issue where Armor Break would not occur properly if the defender was in armor hitstop from another attack
  • Input Buffers
    • Fixed issue where input buffer after blocking an attack was not set correctly for Drive Reversal and some other attacks
  • Modern Controls
    • Fixed issue where S button would not result in damage scaling if a special was input during a Drive Rush screen freeze


Character Changes

A.K.I.

  • 2LP
    • Range increased 0.94 → 0.96
  • 5MP
    • Active frames increased 3 → 5 (better meaty setups, stronger in neutral)


Chun-Li

  • 5LP
    • Hurtbox expanded downward on startup (more easily counterpoked by low-angled attacks)
  • Aerial Lightning Kicks (j.236K)
    • 20% Starter scaling added (less rewarding on Punish Counter when used against throws)


Dee Jay

  • Funky Slicer (214K~LK)
    • Less pushback on block
    • Larger hurtbox during recovery (easier to pressure Dee Jay or whiff punish)


Dhalsim

  • HP Yoga Flame (63214HP)
    • Fixed issue where 2nd hit dealt normal damage on Counter-hit/Punish Counter


Jamie

  • Forward Throw (LPLK)
    • Can now hold LP+LK to gain 1 Drink Level (prevents throw loop, but Jamie is still KD +7)
  • SA1 (236236K)
    • Damage increased by 100 on both the regular version and Drink ender
    • Reduced pushback on Hit in the corner
    • KD Advantage increased by 5 frames (allows Jamie to use Drink ender while maintaining oki)
  • SA2 (214214P)
    • After the install timer ends, Jamie retains his previous Drink Level (no longer resets to DL0)


JP

  • 5HP
    • Expanded foot/torso hurtbox during active and recovery frames
  • 2HP
    • Arm hurtbox is no longer anti-air invincible (more susceptible to trading on late anti-air attempts)
  • Departure (214P/PP)
    • 20% Starter scaling added
    • Increased opponent's Drive gauge recovery on Parry: 5000 → 7500 (regular) / 2500x2 → 3500x2 (OD)
  • Amnesia (22K/KK), successful counter
    • JP no longer builds Super meter while bombs are active or until combo ends
    • Bombs no longer dissipate Super-priority projectiles
    • Starter scaling (when Bomb starts the combo) increased 15% → 50%
    • Immediate scaling (when comboing into Bomb) increased 15% → 60%
    • Canceling an attack into SA3/CA now detonates the Bombs immediately to prevent bypassing this damage scaling


Ken

  • Forward Throw (LPLK)
    • More pushback against cornered opponents (can still walk for a throw loop with ~3f timing window)
  • 5LP
    • Hurtbox expanded downward on startup (more easily counterpoked by low-angled attacks)
  • OD Air Tatsu (j.214KK)
    • Landing recovery on whiff increased 14 → 21 (harder to escape the corner)
    • Collision box expanded so opponents are less likely to jump over this move


Luke

  • 2MP
    • Whiff recovery increased 14 → 17 with a larger hurtbox
  • Aerial Flash Knuckle (j.214P)
    • When performed near the jump apex, has a shorter trajectory and landing recovery on whiff increased 13 → 20
    • Larger feet hurtbox on frame 16 until end of recovery
  • SA1 (236236P)
    • Wider projectile-vulnerable hurtbox on startup frames 1-5


Manon

  • En Haut 1 (4MK)
    • Retains greater momentum out of Drive Rush
  • SA2 (214214K)
    • Juggles more consistently; if opponent drops out, their knockdown period is extended (less likely for Manon to be punished)


Rashid

  • 5LP
    • Hurtbox expanded downward on startup (more easily counterpoked by low-angled attacks)
  • OD Spinning Mixer (236PP)
    • Now has 5 frames of Anti-Air Invuln (9-13f) after Full Invuln frames run out
  • Wall Jump
    • Fixed issue where performing certain moves would not put Rashid into a Forced Knockdown state


Ryu

  • Solar Plexus Strike (6HP)
    • Proximity guard hitbox now reaches the full length of the move's range on frame 2 (much harder for the opponent to walk out of pressure)
  • Hadoken (236P)
    • Reduced the extended arm hurtbox (harder to jump over, counter-poke, or punish with invincible attacks)
  • OD Shoryuken (623PP)
    • First hit now knocks down to prevent Ryu from being punished when the 2nd hit whiffs
  • LP Hashogeki (214LP)
    • Block Advantage -4 → -3 with reduced pushback on block
    • Increased pushback on block vs. Burnout
  • MP Hashogeki (214MP)
    • Expanded the attack and projectile clash hitbox forward
  • HP Hashogeki (214HP)
    • Reduced pushback on block
    • Expanded the attack and projectile clash hitbox forward
  • OD Hashogeki (214PP)
    • Reduced pushback on hit/block
    • Expanded the attack and projectile clash hitbox forward
  • Denjin Hashogeki (Denjin 214P/PP)
    • Reduced pushback on block
    • Expanded the attack and projectile clash hitbox forward
    • Denjin OD version damage increased 600 → 1000
  • SA1 (236236P)
    • Reduced the extended arm hurtbox (harder to jump over or punish with invincible attacks, and more effective against other projectiles)


Zangief

  • OD Double Lariat (PPP)
    • 1st hit combos into SA3/CA when canceled (causes a brief crumple state on grounded opponents)
    • Faster horizontal movement on startup
    • 2nd hit connects vs. grounded opponents 1f earlier (for combo consistency)
    • 1st hit reduced knockback distance vs. airborne opponents
  • SA1 (236236K)
    • Hitbox extended downward (can juggle and anti-air more consistently)
