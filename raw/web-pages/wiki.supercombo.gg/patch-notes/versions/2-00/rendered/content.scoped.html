Game Version2.00
dateAugust 5, 2025
SummarySagat Release. Minor bug fixes and adjustments (mostly related to Tiger Shot interactions).
LinksPatch Notes


Sagat Patch

  • Sagat released


Character Changes

A.K.I.

  • Venomous Fang (2PP~P) shifted the projectile invincible hurtbox downward immediately after startup (to ensure it's possible to attack through a Low Tiger Shot)


Akuma

  • Adamant Flame Follow-up (214P~6P) fixed an issue where the attack could not be performed while a projectile was on the screen
  • Demon Gou Rasen (236KK~j.214K) fixed an issue where the attack could not be performed while a projectile was on the screen
  • SA1 / Air SA1 changed the amount of Drive Gauge gained on Perfect Parry is the same as other Super Arts (20000)


Blanka

  • Backstep Rolling Attack (63214K/KK) fixed an issue where holding forward/back would steer Blanka a different distance depending on whether he was facing left or right


Cammy

  • Spiral Arrow (236K/KK) hurtbox expanded upward at the start and during later active frames (to ensure it cannot easily pass under High Tiger Shot)
    • 236LK: frames 1-2, 14-21
    • 236MK: frames 1-2, 14-23
    • 236HK: frames 1-5, 23-32
    • 236KK: frames 1-4, 19-29
  • SA1 (236236K) upper hurtbox on frames 1-5 that is vulnerable to projectiles expanded upward
    • This ensures that the early startup frames cannot duck under a High Tiger Shot


Chun-Li

  • Critical Art (214214K) fixed an issue where the opponent would gain less Drive Gauge when parrying the attack compared to other Super Arts


Dhalsim

  • 2LK projectile invincible hurtbox shifted downward (to ensure it's possible to slide under Low Tiger Shot with good timing)


Ed

  • LP Psycho Uppercut (623LP) fixed an issue where Ed could cancel the final 4 recovery frames into another action on whiff


E. Honda

  • Neko Damashi (22P) fixed an issue where buffering a SA3/CA input would not work


Elena

  • Round Arch (4HK) fixed an issue where Elena would slide forward when performed out of Drive Rush when not inputting a direction
  • Critical Art (214214K) fixed an issue where the damage value was increased by 1000 when landing a K.O. against the opponent


Kimberly

  • OD Sprint (236KK) Modern Controls (A[6S]) fixed an issue where Down+Forward / Down+Back + Attack would perform Neck Hunter / Emergency Stop instead of Shadow Slide


Luke

  • OD Avenger (236KK) armored head hurtbox shifted downward (to prevent interference with High Tiger Shot)


M. Bison

  • Drive Impact fixed an issue where the armor could absorb 3 or more hits depending on which part of the body they interacted with


Manon

  • Temps Lié (5HP~HP) fixed an issue where Manon could Parry on the final recovery frame on block


Marisa

  • 2MP upper hurtboxes made projectile invincible (to prevent interference with High Tiger Shot)
  • 2MK upper hurtboxes made projectile invincible (to prevent interference with High Tiger Shot)
  • 2HK upper hurtboxes made projectile invincible (to prevent interference with High Tiger Shot)


Rashid

  • 2HP upper hurtboxes made projectile invincible (to prevent interference with High Tiger Shot)
    • Note: the official patch notes incorrectly attributed this change to 2HK



Terry

  • 214LPHP input changed from OD Burning Knuckle to OD Quick Burn
  • SA3/CA (236236P) fixed an issue where the opponent would gain less Drive Gauge compared to other Super Arts on parry or Perfect Parry


Zangief

  • Parry hurtbox from frame 3 onward expanded forward
    • This is to match the hurtbox expansion Zangief received in the June 2025 update
  • 2MP upper hurtboxes made projectile invincible (to prevent interference with High Tiger Shot)
  • Tundra Storm (22HK) counter hitbox expanded upward (allowing it to work against Sagat's 5HK/6HK)
  • Double Lariat (Modern Controls: M+H) reverted the change that allowed L+M+H to perform Double Lariat instead of Throw while in Burnout
    • This is due to a bug that caused the move to come out in unintended situations, and is planned to be reimplemented.
