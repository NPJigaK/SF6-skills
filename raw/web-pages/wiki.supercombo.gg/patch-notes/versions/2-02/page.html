Version2.02
Game Version2.02
dateMarch 17, 2026
SummaryAlex Release. Balance changes for all characters.
LinksPatch Notes


Alex Patch

  • Alex released


Universal Changes

  • Drive Rush Overheads: now universally +1 on Block (-3 normally) with enough block pushback to prevent an immediate Throw or low-hitting light chain (e.g. 2LK~2LP)
    • Only applies to standard overhead command normals that can lead to grounded combos on hit
    • Frame Data changes affect A.K.I., Akuma, Chun-Li, Elena, Ryu, Sagat, Terry
    • Pushback changes affect Blanka, C. Viper, E. Honda, Guile, Jamie, JP, Juri, Kimberly, Lily, Luke, M. Bison, Mai, Marisa, Rashid, Sagat, Terry
  • Drive Rush Screen Freeze: pressing an Up input during the freeze will now buffer a jump after the screen freeze ends
    • Still subject to move priority, like special moves canceling the prejump frames
    • Neutral jumps during the freeze will become forward/back jumps if the left/right direction is held after the Up direction
    • Only applies if the character is in neutral during the screen freeze (will not be buffered on wakeup or during a move's recovery)
  • Wakeup Input Buffer: extended by 3 frames (7f → 10f)


Character Changes

A.K.I.

  • Toxic Blossom builds an additional 2000 Drive meter every time it triggers (unless Drive Rush is used during the combo)
    • 236LP / 236MP / 2PP~P: 2000 → 4000
    • 236HP: 2500 → 4500
    • 5HP~HP / 214LP~6P: 3000 → 5000
    • 236PP / 214PP~6P: 0 → 2000
  • 5LP is now chainable into or out of 2LP/2LK
  • Pu Lao (3MP) blockstun 19 → 18; Block Advantage -2 → -3
  • Hun Dun (5LP~LP) hitstun increased 17 → 18, blockstun increased 15 → 16, recovery increased 14 → 15 (hit/whiff) and 16 → 17 (block); Drive Rush cancel occurs 1f later
    • No change to Hit/Block advantage or DRC advantage; indirectly decreases Perfect Parry advantage -16 → -17 and Sinister Slide cancel advantage +5/+3 → +6/+4
    • These changes allow 214LP to combo from the Target Combo except near max range
  • OD Nightshade Pulse (214PP) 2nd hit Juggle Increase value decreased 3 → 2 (more lenient after juggling on airborne opponents)


Akuma

  • 5MP whiff recovery increased 11 → 13; extended hurtbox lasts until end of recovery
  • 2MK hitstun reduced 23 → 22; Hit Advantage +1 → 0; DRC Hit Advantage +11 → +10
    • Can no longer combo naturally into OD Adamant Flame (214PP)
  • 2HP hitstun increased 27 → 28 on the first 3 active frames; Hit Advantage 0 → +1; DRC Hit Advantage +17 → +18
  • Skull Splitter (6MP) 2nd hit blockstun 20 → 18; Block Advantage -1 → -3
  • MK Tatsumaki Zanku-kyaku (214MK) 2nd hit active frames increased 2 → 3 (and removed 1 airborne recovery frame to compensate)
    • More consistent in juggles where opponent is launched higher up
  • OD Adamant Flame (214PP) startup when canceled from 2MK increased 18 → 20 to prevent it from comboing
  • Lv.3 Gou Hadoken (236[P]/[PP]) hits increased 3 → 4; damage increased 1200 (300x2,600) → 1350 (250x3,600); total chip damage 300 → 336
    • Recovery reduced 28 → 21 (hit/block) / 26 (whiff); total frames are 77(82) meterless / 52(57) OD; "whiff" means the projectile does not make contact within the first 21 active frames
    • Hit Advantage KD +57 → KD +69; Block Advantage +5 → +20; Perfect Parry Advantage +4 → +22; total blockstun 34 → 42
    • Drive Damage increased 3000 → 4000 (Block) / 7000 → 9000 (Punish Counter); Drive Gain (meterless version) increased 1000 → 4000; Super Gain 600 → 800


Blanka

  • 5MK hitstun 24 → 25; Hit Advantage +4 → +5; DRC Hit Advantage +14 → +15
    • Can now combo into Wild Lift (2PP~P) on Counter-hit; reduced pushback so that OD Backstep Roll combos more consistently
  • 2HP now knocks down on Counter-hit, +2 → (KD +40); can only juggle on airborne connect
  • Rock Crusher (6MP) increased pushback on block
  • Wild Nail (6HP) KD Advantage increased KD +23 → KD +30; now KD +2 after ending with Coward Crouch ~ Forward Hop
  • Surprise Forward Hop throw invincibility on frames 12-22 removed
  • SA2-Enhanced Rolling Attack ([4]6P) Drive damage on Block reduced; LP/MP 7500 → 4000, HP 10000 → 6000
  • Blanka-chan Bomb now disappears if Blanka is hit after activating them


C. Viper

  • 2MP active frames 3 → 4 (recovery 17 → 16 to compensate); reduced forward hurtbox on arms during active frames
  • 5HP blockstun 21 → 22; Block Advantage -3 → -2; High Jump > 214HP Feint Block Advantage 0 → +1
  • 5HK increased OTG bounce time and reduced horizontal knockback on airborne opponents
    • Now juggles into corner OD Thunder Dash and is more consistent into OD Burning Kick / SA1 in some routes
  • Viper Elbow (6MP) increased pushback on block
  • HP Thunder Dash (214HP) expanded collision box upward to make it harder for opponents to pass behind C. Viper
  • Aerial Burning Kick (j.236K) blockstun increased 21 → 22 (normal jump) / 20 → 21 (High Jump); landing recovery when cross-up is blocked increased 13 → 14
    • Min/Max Block Advantage increased by +1f when hitting same-side (highly variable); cross-up block advantages are unchanged
    • As a result, High Jump > Air MK Burning Kick is now +1 on Block when used at the earliest timing (powerful approach tool)
  • SA1 expanded hitbox upward on the 1st and 2nd hits
    • Install Timer now causes Tracer Combination / Knuckled Pursuit / Double Burn to build 3000 Drive gauge on hit/block
  • SA2 collision box expanded upward (less likely to whiff under opponent)
    • Input shortcuts no longer apply when using HP button or after High Jump in order to avoid conflict with 214HP Feint execution
    • Also shortened the input buffer window in these scenarios and prevents kara-canceling a normal/special into SA2 unless the full 214214P command is properly input


Cammy

  • Delayed Ripper (6HK) recovery on hit reduced 25 → 20; Juggle Start value reduced 3 → 1; increased juggle height on Punish Counter (KD +48 → +52)
    • Allows new juggle routes, like 6HK > DRC~5HP~HK, or PC 6HK > 214LP, SA1
  • HK Cannon Spike (623HK) increased forward movement on startup (easier to connect the higher-damage early active frames in juggles)
  • Cannon Strike (j.214K/KK) hitstun/blockstun increased by 2f when connecting on the final 2 active frames before landing (causing a screen shake and reduced pushback)
    • Screen shake guarantees a minimum advantage of +10 oH / +4 oB (or +6 oB for OD)
  • SA1 active frames on 1st hit increased 3 → 4 (no difference to total frame count)


Chun-Li

  • Water Lotus Fist (3HP) blockstun 16 → 15; Block Advantage -2 → -3
    • Stance Cancel on Block: -1 → -2 into follow-up, -9 -10 into block
  • Snake Strike (Stance MP) startup reduced 7 → 6; active frames increased 11 → 12; added 1f extra knockdown time on hit (can now combo from 5HP)
    • If the first active frame connects, this makes it 1f more unsafe on block (-15) while keeping the same knockdown advantage
    • Modern Controls: can now hold Up or Down to remain in Serenity Stream stance after recovery
  • Forward Strike (Stance LK) hitbox expanded forward 0.05 units; Range 1.469(1.443) → 1.519(1.493)
  • Lightning Kick Barrage (236KK~KK) no longer has separate damage scaling applied after 236KK hits
  • LK Hazanshu (214LK) damage reduced 1000 → 700 and chip damage 250 → 175; active frames increased 2 → 3; recovery reduced 21 → 20 (no change to total duration)
    • Reduced horizontal range; removed lower body projectile invincibility on frames 7-18; Juggle Limit increased 1 → 5 (best juggle potential of all her meterless specials)
    • Blockstun increased 14 → 18; Block Advantage -9 → -5; increased pushback on block (safe vs. Burnout and can be spaced safely depending on opponent's 5f punish range)
    • Hit effect changed from 0 oH → KD +21 (grounded) and gives a very short ground bounce when juggled on airborne opponents
    • Cancel into SA3 no longer combos due to the knockdown (even with juggled ground bounce); can still be useful for chip damage or for reacting to DI armor
  • HK/OD Hazanshu (214HK/KK) chip damage corrected 250 → 300


Dee Jay

  • 2LK projectile invincibility added to the hurtbox above Dee Jay's foot
  • 2MP hitstun 22 → 23; Hit Advantage +4 → +5; DRC Hit Advantage +12 → +13
    • Now links to 5LK and combos when canceled into MP Machine Gun Uppercut (214MP); DR~2MP links to 5HP on crouching opponents
    • Whiff recovery increased 14 → 15; extended hurtbox lasts until the end of recovery
  • 2HP expanded the front hurtbox during recovery
  • HP Machine Gun Uppercut (214HP) Super Gain on hit increased 2100 → 2500 and increased the input buffer at the end of recovery
  • OD Machine Gun Uppercut (214PP) startup reduced 28 → 26; can now combo from 5HP or 4HK (1st hit) as well as PC/DR 2MP
  • OD Waning Moon (214KK~MK) no longer hits standing opponents on the first active frame (gives consistent +6 advantage on tall characters)
  • Juggling Sway (214K/KK~6P~4P) can now cancel the backdash into ~LK / ~MK / ~HK follow-ups for more mixup potential
    • Reduced the forward hurtbox on frames 5-17 (normal) / 5-13 (OD); moves backward earlier on the OD version


Dhalsim

  • Fixed bug where Drive Parry would drain twice as fast after being held for 30 frames

    • Now cancelable into Drill Kick (j.2K) / Yoga Mummy (j.2LP) on hit (can air-to-air more safely and extend juggle routes after OD Yoga Flame)
    • Increased Juggle Limit 1 → 4 on j.HP / j.HK, 0 → 4 on Drill Kick (j.2K), and 1 → 3 on Yoga Mummy (j.2LP) to capitalize on these juggle conversions
  • MP Yoga Flame (63214MP) expanded the forward hitbox; Drive Gain increased 2400 → 3000
  • HP Yoga Flame (63214HP) expanded the forward hitbox; Drive Gain increased 2700 → 4000
  • OD Yoga Flame (63214PP) recovery on hit reduced 24 → 19; reduced air knockdown time by 4f (results in KD Advantage +50 → +51)
    • Allows Dhalsim to juggle into an immediate j.MP
  • OD Yoga Comet (j.63214PP) Juggle Limit 2,2 → 3,4; Juggle Increase 1,1 → 0,1 (easier to juggle into and more lenient juggle state after comboing into it)
    • Increased the input buffer window after landing


Ed

  • Low Smash Combination (2HK~HP) can be canceled into Kill Rush (6KK/4KK) on hit only; KD Advantage increased +34 → +38 with higher launch

  • MP Hundred Hand Slap (214MP, Normal/Enhanced) startup reduced 18 → 16 (combos from lights); expanded combo-only hitbox forward from 2nd hit onward for juggle consistency

Elena

Jamie

  • Ransui Haze Headbutt (DL4 6HK~4HK~P) attack startup reduced 15 → 14; active frames 4 → 5; Block Advantage -12 → -13; KD Advantage +32 → +31
    • Cancels from the backstep 1f earlier; combined with faster startup, it now frame traps 4-frame buttons after blocked 6HK
  • DL4 Freeflow Strikes 2nd hit (236P/PP~6P) added an extended combo-only hitbox and adjusted pushback to ensure the combo does not drop after the initial hit connects
  • OD Bakkai (236KK) startup reduced 21 → 18; 1st hit active frames increased 3 → 6; recovery decreased 62 → 59
    • Block Advantage -46 → -43; final hit launches opponent higher, KD Advantage +12 → +23; Hitconfirm window 74 → 77


JP

  • Forward Throw distance from opponent increased in the corner; KD Advantage reduced +24 → +23
    • Corner throw loop is now much weaker; still +1 after dashing or can use Drive Rush
  • 2MP extended hurtbox lasts until the end of recovery
  • 5HK Drive Damage on Block reduced 6000 → 4000
  • Guillotinna (6MK) increased pushback on block
  • Zilant (5HK~HP) Drive Damage on Block reduced 2500 → 2000
    • Combined with the 5HK change, this TC drains 0.25 Drive bars less than before
  • MK/HK/OD Torbalan (236MK/HK/KK) extended the vertical detection range for the projectile to transition into its attack to prevent whiffing in juggles
  • Departure: Shadow (214P/PP~214HP) can now be canceled into from 2LK / 5MP / 2MK / 5HK
    • Hitconfirm windows: 5MP 15f / 2MK 15f / 5HK 18f; no combo from 2LK
    • The portal can be slightly farther off-screen while allowing the spike projectile to be activated


Juri

  • Kyosesho (6MP) now knocks down when juggled into on airborne opponents (no difference when not mid-combo)
    • Juggle Increase value 1 → 2 on juggled version; can still only connect in free juggle states (e.g. after 214KK)
  • Senkai Kick (6MK) increased pushback on block
  • Renko Kicks (6HP) whiff recovery increased 20 → 23; extended foot hurtbox lasts until the end of recovery
    • Applies to regular and FSE version
  • LP Tensenrin (623LP) 1st hit has a shorter upward launch; 2nd hit has a higher upward launch on airborne opponents
    • Added larger combo-only hitbox on 2nd hit; increased 2nd hit SA3 cancel window by 4 frames; total Hitconfirm window 17 → 21
    • These changes make it easier to juggle into both hits of 623LP and relaxes the execution requirement for canceling into SA3
  • OD Fuhajin (214KK) Juggle Start value 2 → 0 (free juggle state); reduced horizontal knockback; added 2-hit combo scaling
    • Allows more combo freedom, notably juggling into 6MP which also continues the juggle
  • Ankensatsu (236MK / 236LKHK) blockstun 13 → 15; Block Advantage -10 → -8; reduced forward recovery hurtbox on hit/block
    • Hitbox expands forward gradually to make it easier to connect on late active frames; max range 2.23 → 2.35
    • Regular 236MK only: proximity guard box starts much earlier, 17f → 5f; increased pushback on block; active frames 2-4 result in a knockdown (KD +24~26)
    • 1-Stock 236MK only: causes limited juggle OTG bounce on airborne opponents (mostly useful for canceling to SA3 in juggle routes)
  • Go Ohsatsu (236HK / 236MKHK) expanded the collision box upward to make it less likely to whiff under opponents in juggles
    • Applies to all boosted or non-boosted versions

  • Assist Combo 2 (Modern Controls) updated to:
    • 2MP > 214KK, 6MP > SA1 (with Drive meter and at least 1 Super gauge)

    • DRC Block Advantage +8 → +11; Run~Stop Block Advantage -10 → -7; Drive Damage on Block increased 5000 → 6000
    • 2HP > HK Jinrai is now only a 5 frame gap on block and is DI-safe
  • Run~Shoryuken (KK~623P) 1st hit Juggle Start / Juggle Increase values 1 → 0; 2nd hit Juggle Start 1 → 2 and Juggle Increase 1 → 2
    • Makes it less likely for the 2nd hit to whiff in a juggle when the 1st hit connects
  • Kazekama Shin Kick (236K~6LK) damage increased 500 → 600; Counter-hit/Punish Counter KD Advantage increased +28 → +32 and now causes a Hard Knockdown
  • OD Senka Snap Kick (236KK~6HK) active frames increased 4 → 6; recovery reduced 20 → 18 (no change to total duration)
    • More consistent in juggles when the opponent is launched high from the initial 236KK
  • SA2 now benefits from +20% Counter-hit/Punish Counter damage boost, 2800 → 3360; much stronger as a raw punish


Kimberly

  • Windmill Kick (4HK) increased pushback on block
  • Hidden Variable / Teleport (214P): normals used after reappearing now gain +3 Juggle Limit and cause an air knockdown
    • Gives extended juggles similar to Drive Rush without meter cost, and can give stronger Forced Knockdown punishes
    • Does not apply to 2HK or Command Normals


Lily

  • 5HP startup reduced 11 → 10; head hurtbox invincibility shifted back 1 frame to match (10-35 → 9-34)

  • Ridge Thrust (3HP) increased pushback on block
  • OD Condor Spire (236KK, Regular/Windclad) now has two possible travel distances depending on the button combination
    • Far: MK+HK / Modern: A[3S]
    • Short: LK+MK or LK+HK / Modern: A[1S] or A[2S]
      • Can be more easily spaced on later active frames for improved advantage or to remain safe from Drive Reversal
      • The new Short Windclad version has 8f less KD Advantage compared to the original Far version
    • Regular version: recovery reduced 16 → 13; blockstun 21 → 18 (Block Advantage unaffected); KD Advantage +42(54) → +43(55); Perfect Parry Advantage -27(-15) → -24(-12)
  • Windclad Condor Dive (W.j.PP/PPP) can now spend a Windclad stock to perform a follow-up Condor Dive on the hit/block rebound
    • Can only be performed once per jump; useful to extend combo damage, or punish the opponent trying to dash or Drive Rush to punish the initial Dive
    • The OD version only costs 1 additional Drive bar regardless of which Condor Dive starter was used; grants a Hard Knockdown on hit for great oki anywhere on screen


Luke

  • 5HP recovery on block reduced 23 → 22; Block Advantage -6 → -5; Perfect Parry Advantage -24 → -23

  • Double Impact (6HP~HP) 2nd hit results in a staggered tailspin (Forces Stand) when the 1st hit connects as a Punish Counter
    • Advantage on Punish Counter: +2 (+37 on the cancel frame, allowing a cancel into any fully charged Flash Knuckle); also +28 after DR Cancel
    • Punish Counter version does not incur the usual 2-hit damage scaling when comboed into
  • LP Flash Knuckle (214LP) recovery reduced 31 → 27; KD Advantage increased +32 → +36; blockstun increased 16 → 18; Block Advantage -18 → -12; Perfect Parry Advantage -32 → -28
    • Reduced horizontal knockback on grounded opponents; the final 2 recovery frames on Hit are special/Super cancelable (allows corner juggle into 623LP or SA2)
    • Reduced knockdown advantage on airborne opponents (to prevent unintended combo routes alongside the other changes)
  • Held HP Flash Knuckle (214[HP]) decreased pushback on block for both regular/perfect versions
    • On close range hit, Luke is in range for 2LK~2LP confirm or a 1-frame microwalk throw (longer microwalk if the opponent walks back)


M. Bison

  • Psycho Hammer (6HP) increased pushback on block
  • Psycho Mine Backfist Combo (214P) meterless versions blockstun increased by 3f on all versions; Block Advantage +6 → +9
    • Psycho Mine MP Backfist Combo (214MP) launches the opponent higher (KD +42 → KD +44); can now juggle into 2HK / SA1 / SA3 anywhere on-screen
  • Devil Reverse ([2]8K~P) forward movement speed reduced when activated in front of the opponent
  • SA2 startup reduced 26 → 24; full invincibility reduced 1-32 → 1-30; expanded combo-only hitbox upward
    • Holding back causes shorter horizontal movement, allowing M. Bison to remain on the same side or hit close range opponents more consistently
    • Can now combo from 4HK on the ground


Mai

  • Senkotsu Uchi (6MP) increased pushback on block
  • Falling Kachousen Fan (after 236PP / F.236PP / 236[P] / F.236[P]) hitstun reduced 30 → 25 and blockstun reduced 26 → 21
    • Less time to land combos/mixups/pressure when the bouncing fan connects
    • Does not affect the falling fans after F.236[PP] or Midare Kachousen
  • Held Kachousen (236[P]) recovery increased 30 → 32 and total 62 → 64; 1st hit blockstun reduced 30 → 28
    • 1st Hit Block Advantage -1 → -5; Hit Advantage +1 → -1; Perfect Parry Advantage -20 → -22
    • Can now punish Mai before the bouncing fan connects if used at close range; especially important in corner setups where point blank 236[P] is throw punishable
    • At ranges where only a 5-frame normal punishes, canceling into a forward-moving special can avoid the falling fan (character-specific)
      • The blockstun change also increases the blockstring gap between the 1st fan and bouncing fan; normal-size characters can Throw to punish a close range 236[LP], but tall characters (Dee Jay, JP, Marisa, Sagat, Zangief) will still get Counter-hit by the bouncing fan; they can instead input a crouching Medium/Heavy normal so that Mai is in hitstun/blockstun while the bouncing fan connects, preventing her from getting a combo and pushing her away
  • OD Kachouesen (236PP) 1st hit blockstun reduced 28 → 26; Block Advantage (1st hit) -3 → -5
    • Can now punish Mai before the bouncing fan connects if used at close range; especially important in corner setups where point blank 236PP is throw punishable
    • At ranges where only a 5-frame normal punishes, canceling into a forward-moving special can avoid the falling fan (character-specific)
  • LP Ryuuenbu (214LP, Normal/Flame) expanded hitbox / projectile clash box / proximity guard box forward; now causes Low Crush 7-14f; extends a forward hurtbox on whiff until end of recovery
    • Drive Damage on Block increased 2000 → 4000; Drive Damage on Punish Counter increased 2500 → 6000
    • Makes it a much stronger midrange poking tool overall to compensate for Kachousen nerfs
  • HP Ryuuenbu (214HP, Normal/Flame) 2nd hit launches airborne opponents higher for better juggle follow-ups (e.g. PC 5HK, Dash + 2LP > 214HP, 236LK, corner 623MK)
    • Flame Version: startup reduced 15 → 14 and first hit active frames increased 3 → 4 to allow combo from 5LP (no change to total duration); 1st active frame only connects on grounded opponents
  • Hissatsu Shinobi Bachi (236K) slightly reduced forward movement on hit/block (less corner carry)
  • Musasabi no Mai (j.214P/PP, Normal/Flame) expanded the combo-only hitbox forward on the 7th active frame onward for juggle consistency


Manon

  • 2LK projectile invincibility added to the hurtbox near Manon's head
  • 5HP Juggle Limit increased 0 → 1; now works in more juggle routes into 5HP~HP or SA2
    • e.g. air-to-air j.MP, midscreen 236P~K, corner 236KK, corner 236PP~K > 4HP
  • 5HK proximity guard box starts much later (2f → 12f), allowing opponents to walk back out of range more easily
  • En Haut (4MK~MK) now maintains the Counter-hit/Punish Counter property of the initial 4MK (deals Drive damage for both Punish Counter hits)
    • Can now combo the full TC into 236LP on Counter-hit and 214KK on Punish Counter
    • Drive Rush bonus advantage does not carry though both hits
  • Temps Lié (5HP~HP) active frames increased 2 → 5 (easier to connect in juggles alongside the 5HP change)
  • LK Rond-point (236LK) no longer pushes back on a cornered hit vs. airborne opponents
  • LK Dégagé (214LK) expanded 2nd hit combo-only hitbox for juggle consistency
  • HK Dégagé (214HK) reduced pushback on hit for more consistent Counter-hit/Punish Counter links


Marisa

  • 2MK hitstun increased 24 → 25; hit recovery decreased 18 → 17; Hit Advantage +3 → +5
    • Can now combo out of Drive Rush into 4HP or 5MP~MP (close) / 2MP (mid) / 6MP (far)
    • The mid-range link to 2MP has inconsistent spacing for cancels; 236LK / 236PP / Supers are the best ender
  • 5HK/5[HK] expanded the forward hurtbox during recovery (easier to whiff punish)
  • Novacula 1 (6MP) startup decreased 10 → 9; total animation 31 → 30
    • Now links after DR~2MK from much farther away than Marisa's other link options
  • Malleus Breaker 1 (3HP) increased pushback on block
    • As a result, Malleus Breaker 2 (3HP~3HP) whiffs when the first hit is not charged (likely unintentional)
  • LP Dimachaerus (214LP) added a combo-only hitbox to the 1st active frame for combo consistency (can still whiff from long range cancels)
  • Phalanx (623P) blockstun increased 23 → 24; Block Advantage increased +2(+3) → +3(+4); 623HP pushback on block reduced
    • After blocked MP/HP versions, this improves:
      • Frame traps with 5LP~LP
      • Pressure with 5MP~MP (no longer causes air reset if opponent jumps)
      • Trade combos vs. 4f normals; use 5MP after stand block or 4HP after crouch block for trade setup
  • OD Phalanx (623PP) armor box expanded downward; armor time increased 6-12 → 6-17; pushback on block reduced
    • Marisa is in command throw range (214K/KK~LPLK) against stationary opponents, but cannot convert after 2LK/2MK to threaten a back walk


Rashid

  • 5HK Juggle Start value on Punish Counter reduced 5 → 3; can now juggle into moves like 6MP or 214LK
  • Beak Assault (6HP) increased pushback on block
  • MP Spinning Mixer (236MP) Anti-Air Invincibility increased 6-10 → 6-14 (end of 2nd hit's active frames)
  • OD Whirlwind Shot (236KK) reduced horizontal blowback distance when hitting a grounded opponent
    • Easier to juggle afterward midscreen (can even pick up DR extensions)
  • Held OD Whirlwind Shot (236[KK]) startup reduced 41 → 38; total animation 76 → 73
    • Can now be canceled into Arabian Cyclone follow-ups: Wing Stroke (4K) / Rolling Assault (6K) on frame 55 (can buffer the input as early as frame 37)
    • Roll cancel is -4 oB at point blank, massively + oB from farther; also useful to avoid being punished by a jump-in
    • 3rd hit launches higher when connecting on airborne opponents (easier to juggle follow-ups if opponent lands into the projectile)


Ryu

  • 5MP whiff recovery increased 11 → 13; extended arm/body hurtbox lasts until the end of recovery
  • 2HP 1st active frame extends a combo-only hitbox to have the same range as 2nd active frame (ensures it will be cancelable in grounded combos)
  • Collarbone Breaker (6MP) 2nd hit blockstun 21 → 19; Block Advantage -1 → -3
  • Solar Plexus Strike (6HP) proximity guard box starts much later (3f → 17f), allowing opponents to walk back out of range more easily
  • High Double Strike (5HP~HK) launches the opponent higher (KD +36 → KD +39); can juggle into MP/OD Shoryuken in the corner
    • Denjin Charge cancel KD +7 → KD +10
    • If the HK hits as a Punish Counter (e.g. after Luke's 236KK absorbs the 5HP), Ryu is still KD +37 (very rare scenario)
  • LP Hashogeki (214LP) Drive Damage on Block reduced 4000 → 2000; increased pushback on block vs. Burnout
  • OD Shoryuken (623PP) 1st active frame extends a combo-only hitbox for combo consistency


Sagat

  • 5LK reduced pushback on hit for combo consistency
  • 2MP whiff recovery increased 17 → 19
  • Tiger Heavy Elbow (6MP) blockstun 20 → 19; Block Advantage -2 → -3; increased pushback on block


Terry

  • 2MP whiff recovery increased 14 → 15
  • Hammer Punch (6HP) blockstun → ; Block Advantage -2 → -3; increased pushback on block
  • LK Crack Shoot (214LK) increased pushback on block vs. Burnout (weaker looping pressure)
  • HP Round Wave (236HP) decreased pushback on hit
  • OD Power Charge (236KK) can now cancel into other special moves on hit similar to the HK version
    • Especially useful in the corner where 214HK can stop opponent from bouncing over his head
  • SA1 now causes both players to become fully invincible immediately on contact to prevent unintended projectile interactions
  • SA3/CA startup decreased 3 → 2 when canceled from Rising Tackle, Quick Burn, or Burning Knuckle


Zangief

  • Smetana Dropkick (3HK) lower body hurtbox reduced during from frame 5 onward (stronger Low Crush)
  • OD Double Lariat (PPP) 4th hit expanded downward with increased pushback on block vs. Burnout; 2nd hit blockstun reduced 17 → 14 with reduced pushback on block
    • This allows 3 hits to connect vs. Burnout crouch block for better chip damage, and it can be spaced safely against most punishes due to pushback
    • Does not form a true blockstring between the last 2 hits if the opponent crouch blocks outside of Burnout
