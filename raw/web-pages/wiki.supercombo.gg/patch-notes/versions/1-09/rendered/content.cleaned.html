Game Version1.09
dateFebruary 5, 2025
SummaryMai Release. Minor bug fixes.
LinksPatch Notes


Mai Patch

  • Mai released


Universal Changes

  • Perfect Parry vs. Multi-hit Attacks
    • Re-applied a fix that prevents later hits of a multi-hit move from being canceled after a Perfect Parry
    • This most notably effects Drive Impact punishes against moves like M. Bison's OD Psycho Crusher and Lily's Windclad OD Condor Spire
  • Drive Impact
    • Fixed issue where the input buffer time after recovery was shorter than usual if the DI whiffed after absorbing an attack


Character Adjustments

Akuma

  • Forward Ashura Senku (6KKK)
    • Expanded throw hurtbox backwards to make it harder to avoid throws when passing through opponent


Blanka

  • Drive Reversal
    • No longer moves backward on the first frame


Cammy

  • SA2 / Air SA2 (214214P / j.214214P)
    • Fixed issue where move would not K.O. the opponent when trading hits


Chun-Li

  • 2LK
    • Fixed issue where move could not be canceled into a special when parried by the opponent
  • Tenku Kick (Modern Controls: 214X~2H)
    • Fixed issue where Modern control version had a different jump cancel timing on hit


Dee Jay

  • Threebeat Combo (5LP~MK~MK)
    • Second attack can now come out while holding a downward direction


Ed

  • Psycho Knuckle Lv.1 (5[HP])
    • Fixed issue where opponents could not move backwards after being hit late during this move's active frames


Kimberly

  • Drive Reversal
    • No longer moves backward on the first frame
  • SA1 (236236K)
    • Fixed issue where move would not K.O. the opponent when trading hits
  • SA2 / Air SA2 (214214P / j.214214P)
    • Fixed issue where move would not K.O. the opponent when trading hits


M. Bison

  • Drive Impact
    • Forward movement now begins on frame 2 instead of frame 13
    • Hitbox, hurtbox, armor box, and collision box adjusted to keep overall range and properties similar to before


Manon

  • Rond-point (236K/KK)
    • Fixed issue where the move could not be canceled into a Super when parried by the opponent


Marisa

  • OD Dimachaerus (214PP)
    • Super cancel buffer window shifted forward from frame 2 → frame 10
    • Less likely to get accidental SA2 cancel when inputting 6P follow-up


Rashid

  • SA1 (236236K)
    • Fixed issue where move would not K.O. the opponent when trading hits
    • Fixed issue where knockdown time was much shorter when trading hits


Terry

  • Forward Walk
    • Adjusted animation so that the central axis of the character more closely matches his position (purely a visual change)


Zangief

  • Back Walk
    • Fixed issue where the first frame moved forward during Burnout
