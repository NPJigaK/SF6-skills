Game Version1.02
dateSeptember 27, 2023
SummaryA.K.I. Release. Bug fixes with some minor changes due to bugs
LinksPatch Notes


A.K.I. Patch

  • A.K.I. released


Universal Changes

  • Forward/Back Dash
    • Input buffer window increased 4 → 7 frames (easier to get a dash with reversal timing)
  • Super Arts
    • Input recognition window reduced to 9 frames between inputs (less likely to come out accidentally)
  • Drive Gain / Chip Damage vs. Burnout
    • Several attacks unintentionally built less Drive gauge or did incorrect Chip damage when the opponent blocked while in Burnout and have been adjusted to the standard values
  • Drive Impact
    • Fixed issue where a DI Clash would occur in specific situations after an attack was absorbed by armor (canceling each other out)
    • e.g. a cancelable poke is absorbed on the first DI armor frame and immediately canceled into a counter-DI
  • Drive Parry
    • Fixed issue where performing Parry from a crouching position would cost more Drive meter than from a standing position
    • Also fixed issue where Dee Jay, E. Honda, Guile, Kimberly, Luke, Rashid, and Zangief would spend extra Drive meter to initiate a crouching Parry
  • Perfect Parry
    • Drive Gain when parrying multi-hit projectiles adjusted to prevent the defender from consuming more Drive meter than they spent
    • Fixed an issue where inputting any upward direction after Perfect Parrying a projectile would buffer a jump input after the parry recovery ends
    • Fixed an issue where after a jump normal was Perfect Parried, the timing to input an attack after landing was 1 frame later than usual
  • Attacks hitting backwards
    • Many attacks could unintentionally hit opponents behind the player and have been adjusted to only hit in front
  • Assist Combos (Modern)
    • Adjusted the timing that inputs are accepted and fixed buffer window issues
    • Can no longer chain into a Light normal while holding the Assist button (allows Light Assist Combo to take priority)
