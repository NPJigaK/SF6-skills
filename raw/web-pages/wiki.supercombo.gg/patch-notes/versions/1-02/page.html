< Street Fighter 6
Version1.02
Game Version1.02
dateSeptember 27, 2023
SummaryA.K.I. Release. Bug fixes with some minor changes due to bugs
LinksPatch Notes


A.K.I. Patch

  • A.K.I. released


Universal Changes

  • Forward/Back Dash
    • Input buffer window increased 4 → 7 frames (easier to get a dash with reversal timing)
  • Super Arts
    • Input recognition window reduced to 9 frames between inputs (less likely to come out accidentally)
  • Drive Gain / Chip Damage vs. Burnout
    • Several attacks unintentionally built less Drive gauge or did incorrect Chip damage when the opponent blocked while in Burnout and have been adjusted to the standard values
  • Drive Impact
    • Fixed issue where a DI Clash would occur in specific situations after an attack was absorbed by armor (canceling each other out)
    • e.g. a cancelable poke is absorbed on the first DI armor frame and immediately canceled into a counter-DI
  • Drive Parry
    • Fixed issue where performing Parry from a crouching position would cost more Drive meter than from a standing position
    • Also fixed issue where Dee Jay, E. Honda, Guile, Kimberly, Luke, Rashid, and Zangief would spend extra Drive meter to initiate a crouching Parry
  • Perfect Parry
    • Drive Gain when parrying multi-hit projectiles adjusted to prevent the defender from consuming more Drive meter than they spent
    • Fixed an issue where inputting any upward direction after Perfect Parrying a projectile would buffer a jump input after the parry recovery ends
    • Fixed an issue where after a jump normal was Perfect Parried, the timing to input an attack after landing was 1 frame later than usual
  • Attacks hitting backwards
    • Many attacks could unintentionally hit opponents behind the player and have been adjusted to only hit in front
  • Assist Combos (Modern)
    • Adjusted the timing that inputs are accepted and fixed buffer window issues
    • Can no longer chain into a Light normal while holding the Assist button (allows Light Assist Combo to take priority)


Key Character Changes

Blanka

  • 2HK
    • Super Gain on Block decreased 600 → 500 (corrected to standard value)
  • Rock Crusher (6MP)
    • Super Gain on Block decreased 250,250 → 125,125 (corrected to standard value)
  • Double Knee Bombs (6MK)
    • Super Gain on Block decreased 250,250 → 125,125 (corrected to standard value)
  • OD Aerial Rolling Attack (j.[4]6PP)
    • Super Gain on Block for the 1st hit decreased 650 → 450 (corrected to standard value)
  • OD Backstep Rolling Attack (63214KK)
    • Increased opponent's Drive Gain on Parry 5000 → 10000 (1st attack) / 2500 → 5000 (2nd/3rd attacks)
  • Universal Adjustments - Attacks Hitting Behind
    • 5HK, 6MK, 63214KK (1st hit), [4]6LP (during SA2), SA3
  • Universal Adjustments - Perfect Parry Drive Gain
    • 6MP, 6MK, 63214KK


Cammy

  • Lift Uppercut (4MP)
    • Later active frames will no longer connect on crouch blocking opponents when timed meaty on the opponent's wakeup
  • SA2 / Air SA2 (214214P Ground/Air)
    • Decreased the Drive Damage on a cinematic hit 11000 → 10000
    • Increased the Drive Damage on the final non-cinematic airborne hit 3000 → 3700 (can now match the cinematic version if all hits connect)
  • SA2 / Air SA2 / SA3/CA
    • Fixed issue where a stunned opponent would recover from Burnout mid-combo if a Super connected while their Drive gauge was full
  • Universal Adjustments - Drive Gain vs. Burnout
    • 6HK, 5HP~HK, 236P/PP~No Input, 236P/PP~2K
  • Universal Adjustments - Perfect Parry Drive Gain
    • 236HK/[HK]/KK, 236[HK]/KK~No Input, 236[HK]/KK~2K, SA1, SA2, Air SA2


Chun-Li

  • Assist Combo 2
    • Fixed issue where after performing SA2, the next hit would not come out at if input at a specific timing
  • j.HP
    • Corrected the Counter-hit/Punish Counter damage vs. airborne opponents 960 → 600 (was using the damage value for grounded opponents)
  • Lotus Punch (214P~HP)
    • Removed unintended invincibility on head hurtbox on frames 3-28
    • 2nd hit is now properly considered an overhead if the 1st hit whiffs
  • OD Tenko Kicks (22KK)
    • Fixed issue where Chun-Li would move after landing if the opponent switched sides on the 2nd hit of the attack
  • SA1 (236236P)
    • Fixed issue where only the final hit could KO against an airborne opponent
  • SA3/CA (214214K)
    • The distribution of the Drive Damage on Block has been adjusted
    • SA3: 7500,0,0 → 6500,500,500
    • CA: 10000,0,0 → 9000,500,500
  • Universal Adjustments - Attacks Hitting Behind
    • 236K/KK, 236KK~KK, 214K/KK, [2]8K/KK, SA3
  • Universal Adjustments - Chip Damage vs. Burnout
    • [2]8MK
  • Universal Adjustments - Drive Gain vs. Burnout
    • 214P~HP
  • Universal Adjustments - Perfect Parry Drive Gain
    • 2HP, 214P~2HP, 236K/KK, 236KK~KK, j.236K/KK, [2]8K/KK, Drive Reversal, SA1, Air SA1, SA2, SA3


Dee Jay

  • Modern Controls
    • Air Slasher shortcut ([4]S) will now only come out while still holding Back (4) or Up-Back (7)
    • The short period where charge time is retained after letting go of the backward input no longer applies except when manually inputting Air Slasher
    • This prevents overlap issues with Rolling Sobat and Jus Cool, as the Air Slasher would take priority for a short while after releasing charge
  • Assist Combo 1
    • Fixed issue where Dee Jay could not perform 5LK > 236MK by inputting it as A[L] > 6S
  • Assist Combo 3
    • Fixed issue where the 3rd hit of the combo would have extra damage scaling if input late
  • Speedy Maracas (22P)
    • Now in a Punish Counter state when transitioned into from Air Slasher (to avoid confusion when punishing the projectile)
    • Can no longer transition from Air Slasher into Speedy Maracas when the projectile hits as a Counter-hit/Punish Counter, is parried, or is absorbed by armor
  • Drive Reversal
    • Updated the hurtbox to match the visuals more closely
  • Universal Adjustments - Attacks Hitting Behind
    • 5LK, 214P (1st hit), SA2, SA3
  • Universal Adjustments - Chip Damage vs. Burnout
    • 214P/PP, 236MK, 236HK, [2]8MK, [2]8HK
  • Universal Adjustments - Drive Gain vs. Burnout
    • 214K/KK~LK, 214K/KK~MK,
  • Universal Adjustments - Perfect Parry Drive Gain
    • 4HK, 236HK, 236KK, SA2 (M/H versions)


Dhalsim

  • 5MK
    • Super Gain on Punish Counter decreased 700 → 500 (corrected to standard value)
  • j.MK
    • Corrected the tip of the hitbox so that it must be blocked as an Overhead instead of both Low / High
  • j.HP
    • Fixed issue where opponents in Burnout did not incur 4 extra frames of blockstun
  • HP Yoga Flame (63214HP)
    • Fixed issue where only 2 projectile clash hits would occur instead of 3
  • Yoga Arch (236K)
    • Fixed issue where Starter / Combo Scaling would be applied when the stationary hitbox connected (after the projectile hits the ground)
  • OD Yoga Comet (j.63214PP)
    • Fixed issue where activating the move with plinked inputs (P~P) would prevent Dhalsim from using other airborne specials after recovery
  • Aerial Yoga Teleport (4 or 6 + j.PPP/j.KKK)
    • Fixed issue where Dhalsim would gain Super gauge when activated
    • Fixed issue where the PPP version would take 1 frame longer to land than the KKK version if nothing was input mid-air
  • SA2 (214214K)
    • Fixed issue where the projectile became invisible if only the final hit did not connect
  • Universal Adjustments - Perfect Parry Drive Gain
    • 63214PP, Drive Reversal, SA1, SA2


E. Honda

  • 5HP
    • Fixed issue where Drive Rush + Sumo Spirit enhancement traveled a much farther distance than usual
  • 2HP
    • Fixed issue where Drive Rush + Sumo Spirit enhancement traveled a much shorter distance than usual
  • Universal Adjustments - Drive Gain vs. Burnout
    • 6HK, 3HK, 5MP~3HK, [2]8K/KK (2nd hit)
  • Universal Adjustments - Perfect Parry Drive Gain
    • 214P/PP, [4]6PP, 236K/KK~P, 236K/KK~2P, SA2, SA3


Guile

  • 2LK
    • Fixed issue where it could not be chain canceled when absorbed by armor
  • Double Shot (2MP~2MP)
    • Fixed issue where the extra Block Advantage vs. Burnout was +5 instead of +4
  • OD Sonic Cross (214PP)
    • Decreased opponent's Drive Gain on Parry 5100 → 5000
  • SA1 ([4]646P)
    • Decreased opponent's Drive Damage on Block 2520 → 2500
    • Fixed issue where HP version's projectile clash did not match the visuals
  • SA3/CA ([4]646K)
    • Fixed issue where a stunned opponent would recover from Burnout mid-combo if SA3/CA connected while their Drive gauge was full
  • Universal Adjustments - Perfect Parry Drive Gain
    • [2]8KK, SA1


Jamie

  • Forward/Back Walk
    • Fixed issue where Jamie would not move on the first frame during Drink Lv.4
  • Tensei Kick (2KK)
    • Fixed issue where startup was 1 frame slower while SA2 Install is active
  • Ransui Haze Drink (6HK~4HK~P Far)
    • Fixed issue where Jamie's damage scaling would not increase properly when the drink occurs after SA2 Install runs out
  • Universal Adjustments - Attacks Hitting Behind
    • 5LK (chained), SA1, SA3
  • Universal Adjustments - Drive Gain vs. Burnout
    • 2MK
  • Universal Adjustments - Perfect Parry Drive Gain
    • 5HP, 2HK, 6HK~4HK~P (Close), 236P (DL4), 236K, SA1, SA3


JP

  • OD Embrace (214KK)
    • Fixed issue where JP could move freely if the throw connected at the same time he was hit by a projectile
  • HP Stribog (236HP, 1st hit)
    • Fixed issue where JP would only gain half the usual amount of Drive Gauge on block when the opponent is in Burnout
  • SA1 (236236P)
    • When both hits are parried, Drive Gain on Parry distribution changed from 0,20000 → 10000,10000
  • SA2 (214214P)
    • Fixed an issue where the final projectile would come out if JP was hit by an attack with short hitstun
  • SA3/CA (236236K)
    • Fixed issue where a stunned opponent would recover from Burnout mid-combo if SA3/CA connected while their Drive gauge was full
  • Universal Adjustments - Attacks Hitting Behind
    • 5LK, 236P
  • Universal Adjustments - Drive Gain vs. Burnout
    • 236MK, 236HK, SA2
  • Universal Adjustments - Perfect Parry Drive Gain
    • 6HK, SA1, SA2


Juri

  • Modern Controls
    • Fixed issue where performing 2L > Assist Combo 1 would result in a chained light normal during FSE (SA2 Install)
  • Assist Combo 3
    • Fixed issue where the transition from OD Ankensatsu to OD Go Ohsatsu would cost 2 Drive bars instead of 1
  • FSE 6MK
    • Fixed issue where Drive Gauge values were not scaled like other FSE-chained attacks
    • Drive Gain on Hit 500 → 375
    • Drive Damage on Block 1250 → 625
    • Drive Damage on Punish Counter 2000 → 1250
  • Universal Adjustments - Attacks Hitting Behind
    • SA2 (initial hit)
  • Universal Adjustments - Perfect Parry Drive Gain
    • 5MK, 6HK, 4HK, 623P/PP, 236HK (1-stock), 236MKHK, SA1


Ken

  • Drive Reversal
    • Fixed issue where Ken's invincibility lasted longer on hit than other characters
  • Run~Shoryuken (KK~623P)
    • Super Gain on Block decreased 300 → 200 for the 1st/2nd hits (corrected to standard value)
  • OD Hadoken (236PP)
    • Drive Damage on Punish Counter adjusted 5000,0 → 2500,2500
  • Universal Adjustments - Attacks Hitting Behind
    • KK~HK, KK~214K (forward hitboxes), 236K/KK, 214MK, 214HK, 623K
  • Universal Adjustments - Chip Damage vs. Burnout
    • 623LK
  • Universal Adjustments - Drive Gain vs. Burnout
    • KK~MK, 236K/KK~6LK, 236K/KK~6MK
  • Universal Adjustments - Perfect Parry Drive Gain
    • 623MP/HP/PP, KK~623P, 214MK/HK/KK, KK~214K, SA2, SA3


Kimberly

  • Bushin Prism Strikes 1 (5LP~MP)
    • Super Gain on Block increased 150 → 200 (corrected to standard value)
  • MP/HP/OD Vagabond Edge (236MP/HP/PP)
    • Corrected the Drive Damage on Block 2000 → 4000
  • Super Arts (all)
    • Fixed issue where a stunned opponent would recover from Burnout mid-combo if a Super connected while their Drive gauge was full
  • Universal Adjustments - Drive Gain vs. Burnout
    • 236K/KK~MK, 236K/KK~HK
  • Universal Adjustments - Perfect Parry Drive Gain
    • j.214K/KK, 236K/KK (Close), 236KK~LK, 236KK~MK, 236KK~HK


Lily

  • Prejump Frames
    • Fixed issue where Lily could cancel prejump frames into special moves 1 frame longer than intended (except for 360+P command throws)
  • SA2 / Air SA2 (236236K / j.236236K)
    • Increased the Drive Gain on Parry for the Windclad versions to match the regular versions
    • Expanded a combo-only hitbox upward for juggle consistency
  • Universal Adjustments - Drive Gain vs. Burnout
    • j.2HP
  • Universal Adjustments - Perfect Parry Drive Gain
    • 2HP, 2HK, 623PP, W.623P/PP, j.PPP, W.j.PP/PPP, SA1, SA2, Air SA2


Luke

  • OD No Chaser (236KK~P)
    • Super Gain on Block increased 500 → 600 (corrected to standard value)
  • Drive Impact
    • Fixed issue that would cause the opponent to take no damage when being hit in certain circumstances
  • Universal Adjustments - Chip Damage vs. Burnout
    • pf.214[LP] (2nd hit)
  • Universal Adjustments - Drive Gain vs. Burnout
    • pf.214[LP] (2nd hit), 236PP~PP
  • Universal Adjustments - Perfect Parry Drive Gain
    • 214[P] (Perfect/non-Perfect), 623PP, SA1, SA2


Manon

  • Drive Impact
    • Fixed issue where DI Clash would occur even if activated 1 frame later than the opponent
  • Rond-point (236K)
    • HK version 2nd hit Punish Counter damage corrected 600 → 480
    • HK/OD version 2nd hit recovery no longer cancelable early on block
  • OD Renversé (236PP)
    • Fixed issue where recovery was 1 frame slower at Medal Lv.3 or Lv.4
  • SA1 (236236K)
    • Fixed issue where Manon could pass through some moves while both are active
  • Universal Adjustments - Attacks Hitting Behind
    • 5LK, SA3
  • Universal Adjustments - Drive Gain vs. Burnout
    • SA1
  • Universal Adjustments - Perfect Parry Drive Gain
    • 214LK, 214MK, 236K, SA2


Marisa

  • Modern Controls
    • Fixed issue where normals could not be canceled into Gladius using 6S input
    • 6S is normally Scutum (214K) which cannot be canceled into from normals, so the 6S acts like a 5S in this context
  • Caelum Arc (nj.2HP)
    • Fixed issue where the recovery was much longer if it connected as a cross-up; Hit Recovery 27 → 19; Block Recovery 23 → 15
    • Fixed issue where the opponent would turn the wrong way if blocked cross-up during Burnout
    • Fixed issue where the landing recovery could be canceled into with another move
  • Dimachaerus (214P)
    • Fixed issue where Super Gain on Block was not half of the standard Hit value
  • Held SA1 (236236[P])
    • Fixed issue where the damage increased on Counter-hit/Punish Counter on active frames 5-6
  • Universal Adjustments - Attacks Hitting Behind
    • 236P, 623P, 236K
  • Universal Adjustments - Drive Gain vs. Burnout
    • 2HK, 2[HK], j.[HP], j.[HK], nj.2[HP], 6MP~HP
  • Universal Adjustments - Perfect Parry Drive Gain
    • SA2


Rashid

  • Forward Dash
    • Adjusted the beginning of the animation to be more visually apparent
    • Fixed issue where the total dash duration would change depending on how the move was input
    • Fixed issue where Rashid could not input a Throw Escape after the dash recovery frames had ended
  • Air Throw (j.LPLK)
    • Drive Gain on Hit increased 1600 → 2000
  • Enhanced Tempest Moon (6[6]~6K Air)
    • Drive Gain on Parry adjusted 10000,5000,5000 → 5000,2500,2500
    • Previously would build 2 bars for the opponent instead of the standard 1 bar
  • Drive Impact
    • Fixed issue that would cause the opponent to take no damage when being hit in certain circumstances
  • Enhanced LP Spinning Mixer (236LP Air)
    • Drive Gain on Block adjusted 2000 → 900 to match the value on Hit
  • Enhanced MP Spinning Mixer (236MP Air)
    • Damage on Punish Counter corrected to the standard +20% bonus: 2880 (360x2,240x8,240) → 1680 (240x2,120x8,240)
    • Drive Gain on Punish Counter corrected to match the value on Hit: 0x2,200x8,200 → 150x10,500
  • Enhanced OD Spinning Mixer (236PP Air)
    • Damage on Punish Counter corrected to the standard +20% bonus: 2880 (360x2,240x8,240) → 1680 (240x2,120x8,240)
    • Super Gain on Punish Counter for 3rd hit adjusted 100 → 50 (corrected to standard value)
    • Drive Gain on Parry 12000 → 10000
    • Fixed issue where Rashid would gain Drive meter on Punish Counter from the 4th hit onward
  • Enhanced OD Eagle Spike (214KK Air)
    • Fixed issue where Rashid would whiff through a grounded character if SA2 was placed behind the opponent
  • OD Arabian Skyhigh (j.214KK)
    • Fixed issue where Rashid would gain Drive meter when blocked
  • Super Gain on Block (corrected to be 1/2 the standard value on Hit)
    • 6MP, 6[6]~6K, Enhanced 236LP/HP, Enhanced 236MP (vs. stand block), 214MK/HK, 214KK (vs. Burnout), Enhanced 214MK/HK, 214P, 214P/PP~6K~K, j.214K/KK
  • Universal Adjustments - Chip Damage vs. Burnout
    • Enhanced 236HP, Enhanced 236PP, 236[MK] (1st hit), 214PP, 214P/PP~6K~K, j.214K
  • Universal Adjustments - Perfect Parry Drive Gain
    • j.HP, 6MP, 6[6]~6K, 236LP/MP/PP, 214P/PP, SA2


Ryu

  • 2HP
    • Fixed issue where Ryu would move backwards if the 2nd active frame connected on hit
  • Fuwa Triple Strike (5MP~LK~HK)
    • Fixed issue where 2nd hit would increase the opponent's Drive gauge on Punish Counter; now decreases it by 2000 instead
    • 3rd hit Drive Damage on Punish Counter increased 0 → 10000
  • High Double Strike (5HP~HK)
    • 2nd hit Drive Damage on Punish Counter increased 0 → 10000
  • SA2 Lv.2/3 (214214[P])
    • Hitbox expanded inwards; collision box expanded upwards from button release until the end of active frames
    • Only applies mid-combo to prevent the attack from whiffing under an opponent
  • Universal Adjustments - Attacks Hitting Behind
    • 5LK, 5MP~LK, SA3 (final hit)
  • Universal Adjustments - Drive Gain vs. Burnout
    • j.MP (2nd hit), 6MP (2nd hit)
  • Universal Adjustments - Perfect Parry Drive Gain
    • j.MP, 6MP, 6HP, 4HK, 623P, 214MK/HK/KK, SA1, SA3


Zangief

  • Smetana Dropkick (3HK)
    • Drive Damage on Block increased 3000 → 6000
  • Borscht Dynamite (j.360+K)
    • Input Recognition Time: 20f → 12f (less likely to come out accidentally mid-air)
    • Super Gain on Punish Counter increased by 150 (corrected to match the standard value)
  • OD Double Lariat (PPP)
    • Super Gain on Hit decreased 300 → 250 (2nd hit onward)
  • SA1 (236236K)
    • Fixed issue where Drive Damage on Hit was 60 less when input with Modern shortcut
    • Fixed issue where a stunned opponent would recover from Burnout mid-combo if SA1 connected while their Drive gauge was full
  • SA2 (236236P)
    • 2nd hit onward: expanded the collision box and hitboxes vertically on a successful hit to prevent a juggle from dropping
    • Fixed issue where the suction effect would persist when interrupted on startup
  • Universal Adjustments - Attacks Hitting Behind
    • 5LK
  • Universal Adjustments - Drive Gain vs. Burnout
    • 2MK, 3HK
  • Universal Adjustments - Perfect Parry Drive Gain
    • PP/PPP, SA2
